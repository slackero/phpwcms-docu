The organization of articles in structure levels

In this part you will learn how simple it is to organize articles, i.e. copy or move of articles and content parts. So it is possible to reuse articles or articles parts at another place.

Alternatively if you want to reuse an article at another place it is also possible to use the content part "alias article" or if it is a content part to use the content part "content part alias". This makes sense when the article/content part changes from time to time, because then the changes will also be taken over in the alias articles and alias content parts.
