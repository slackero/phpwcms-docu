With this replacement tag you are able to show the creation date of an article.

DOWNLOAD HERE

The following notations are possible:

{WAK_ARTICLE_CREATED}
2005/01/09

{WAK_ARTICLE_CREATED:SHORT}
2005/01/09

{WAK_ARTICLE_CREATED:LONG} 
Sunday, 9. January 2005

{WAK_ARTICLE_CREATED:MEDIUM}
Sun, 9. Jan 05

{WAK_ARTICLE_CREATED:ARTICLE}
2005/01/09 

{WAK_ARTICLE_CREATED:SHORT|LONG|MEDIUM|ARTICLE:<ARTICLE_ID>}
These example shows the article with the ID 2 in an "short" notation.
{WAK_ARTICLE_CREATED:SHORT:2}
2004/08/16



To change the date language and format please check this link.  Languagesettings.
