Content Part "Link Article"

link article (optional): Here will appear all the articles you select, chosen from amongst those which are available to visitors of the website.

articles: Here all of the website’s articles are listed. Click on an article to select it, and hold the [Ctrl] key while clicking to select multiple articles (on a Macintosh, hold the [closed apple] key)

TIP: Unfortunately in some browsers the list is not sorted automatically. Click once on the  symbol, in order to sort the list alphabetically. 

With this button you can add the articles you’ve selected from the list into the “link article" box. 

With this button you can remove selected articles from the box, "link article". 

With this button you can shift selected articles upward a position in the "link article" box. 

With this button you can shift selected articles downward a position in the "link article" box.
