Welcome to phpwcms-docu

We are pleased to welcome you to the project "Documentation for the Web Content Management System phpwcms.

This website is targeted both to the beginner and the lateral entry following the goal to simplify the work with phpwcms and to focus on the essentials things.

Therefore we see this project as a kind of compass that will show you the possibilities of phpWCMS and guide you to your readymade web application without any hassle. You will find further answers to questions at: Forum.

When you are still searching for an adequate system und you could still not decide what cms to take, we want  to give you some small hints finding a suitable solution.

Why should phpwcms be used at your web presence?

phpwcms features the following advantages
(only to name some):

  • Separation of content and design through the use of templates
  • Small costs for the content maintanance
  • Simple relaunch-possibility
  • Actuality through a simple publication
  • A higher attractiveness through dynamic additional services
  • Simple enhancement of the web presence without specialized knowledge
  • Individual workflow management
  • Standardized appearance of the website (Corporate Design)

Furthermore it is intuitive as well for editors as for administrators and usable without a long learning curve. Because the administration of your website will be exclusively made using an internet browser, no additional software will be needed and you maintain your website using a laptop no matter whether you are in the office or you are on the way with a laptop.

This website will give you the appropriate documentation for this great cms.  Please consider that this project is made by volunteers in their spare time and not all parts of the content are described. Suggestions, critic or praise are always welcome. Simply use our contact form or send your email to info@phpwcms-docu.de. Your entry in out guestbook welcome as well.
