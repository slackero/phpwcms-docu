Login to Backend

Overview and preparation

click to enlarge
PhpWCMS distinguishs oneself because of the easy handling, clear structure and the straight separation of administration and editorial office.

First, before you can add or edit contents, you have to login. Please type your adress like "http://mydomain.com/login.php" -
or if phpwcms is installed in a subfolder -,
http://mydomain.com/subfolder/login.php in your browser. Now you should see this login-panel: 

When you installed phpwcms by your own, please use the datas of your first created account.

click to enlarge
After the login you will see the administration-level: 

On the left side you see the all loged in user who are working in this administration-level. ( in this case "editor") In the red strip you will see the main menue with the items " article", " file", "Message" "chat" and "profile". We will explain therse later. Furthermore you have with "logout" the option to leave the administration-level every time you want to. 


If you logged in with administration rights (f.i. directly after the installation) you will see in the item Admin where the structure and design of your website is fixed.
