Content Part "RSS Feed"

Here you can integrate RSS feeds from other sites into phpwcms.
If you don't know what RSS feeds are, read here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RSS

Field descriptions
template:

Here you can choose the template for the display of the feeds.
You can find the templates here:
/phpwcms_template/inc_cntpart/rssfeed/default.html

RSS url:

Insert here the URL to the RSS feed. For example: If you want to show the RSS feed by Heise Verlag, insert the following address: http://www.heise.de/newsticker/heise.rdf

items:

Here you can define how many entries will be shown.
hide 1st item:When you choose this option, the first entry of the feed will be suppressed.
cache und timeout:
Here you decide whether the entries are also written into the cache or not. Furthermore you can define how long they are written into the cache. With a click onto the dropdown menu you can define the time. Possible values are from 1 minute to 1 month. The standard value is the time that you also have defined in the file conf.inc.php in line 74.
