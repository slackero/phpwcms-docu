Newsletter example

Here you see a example of a newsletter. You can use it by "copy & paste".



info text:

The e-mail newsletter is without any obligation. No data are transmitted nor used for any marketing purpose, After sending your registration you will receive an e-mail with the request of confirmation. With this confirmation you will release your newsletter abo.



success text:

Thank you,

Your e-mail address has been added to the list. You should receive the confirmation e-mail within seconds. Please execute the relating link to be unlocked as authorized recipient of our newsletter. There you also find the possibility to delete your e-mail address from our database.
