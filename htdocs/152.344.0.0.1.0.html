Content Part „WYSIWYG HTML“

content part "WYSIWYG HTML"

The content part "WYSIWYG HTML" is the most used CP on a website together with the CP "Text with Images".

WYSIWYG means "What You See Is What You Get". That means that all that you insert here will be shown in the same way for your visitors.

phpwcms contains at default 2 WYSIWYG editors. These are Spaw 2 und FCKeditor.

The default editor in conf.inc.php is FCKeditor.

At any time you can change the editor in your profile settings.
