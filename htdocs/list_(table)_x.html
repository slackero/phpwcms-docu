Content Part "List (Table)"

content part "list (table)"

List (table): Here you can enter individual list-items, which will comprise a simple bulleted list. Enter each list-item on a new line.

Below you will see an example, where we added 6 rows.

list item 1
list item 2
list item 3
list item 4
list item 5
list item 6

The Image you see at the begin of each row , you will find on your webspace / server at  "img/article/bullet_1.gif".
