Copy structure / level

Follow these steps to copy and paste a category:

In the site structure overview, click to choose the category to be copied.

Click again to cancel the process.
 

will now appear to indicate where you can paste the category/structure.

Click to paste the entire category, including any sub categories and articles, at a new location.

Note:

The aliases for the categories will not be copied over.


Finalise the process by clicking  beside the originally for copying selected category.
