Messages

click to enlarge
You can send in the administration level messages to other users. The handling of this function is really easy and clear. When you select the massage function, you get immediately a overview of your new, already read, deleted or created massages. Click for that only on the links for “new”, “old”.”sent” or “deleted”. The number in front of the link shows the amount of messages in the folder.
